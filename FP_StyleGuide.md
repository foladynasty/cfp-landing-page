## 1. BRAND IDENTITY

### Who We Are
Financial Perspectives (FP) is Singapore's leading CFP® education provider, established in 1999. We are the first FPAS-approved Education Provider in Singapore and a longstanding IBF-accredited training partner. As of 31 December 2025, FP has trained over 30,000 financial professionals across Asia.

### Brand Positioning Statement
> *Financial Perspectives is the centre of excellence for financial education and professional certification in Singapore — empowering financial professionals with the competencies, ethics, and confidence to transform their clients' lives.*

### Brand Pillars
| Pillar | What It Means in Practice |
|---|---|
| **Authority** | We are the market leader. Every piece of content should reinforce expertise and track record. |
| **Accessibility** | We make professional certification achievable — financially (IBF funding) and practically (flexible formats). |
| **Integrity** | We speak plainly, honestly, and never oversell. No gimmicks. |
| **Transformation** | We believe education changes careers and lives. Copy should carry this conviction. |

### Tagline
> *Centre for Professional Excellence in Advisory Competency and Ethics*

This tagline appears beneath the FP logo in all formal contexts. It should not be altered or abbreviated.

## 2. LOGO USAGE

### Primary Logo
- The FP monogram (block "FP" in a square badge) with the full name and tagline
- Always use the approved logo file — do not recreate or redraw
- Minimum size: 120px wide on digital; 30mm wide in print
- Clear space: maintain padding equal to the height of the "F" character on all sides

### Logo Colour Variants
| Variant | When to Use |
|---|---|
| Full colour (Navy + Gold) | Default — white or light backgrounds |
| Reversed (White + Gold) | Dark/navy backgrounds |
| Monochrome Navy | Single-colour print |
| Monochrome White | Dark single-colour backgrounds |

### What NOT to Do
- ❌ Do not stretch, skew, or rotate the logo
- ❌ Do not change the logo colours
- ❌ Do not place the logo on a busy photographic background without a clear background box
- ❌ Do not use the FP monogram alone without the full wordmark except in app icons or favicons
- ❌ Do not add drop shadows or effects to the logo

- **Never** use more than 3 colours in a single layout section
- Gold is always an **accent** — never use it as a large background fill except in callout banners
- Ensure **WCAG AA contrast** minimum for all text:
  - White text on FP Navy ✅
  - FP Navy text on White ✅
  - FP Navy text on Cream ✅
  - Gold text on White ⚠️ — use sparingly, large text only
  - White text on Gold ✅ for buttons

| Element | Size |
|---|---|
| Hero Headline | 32–40px |
| Section Headline | 24–30px |
| Body Text | 16px (never below) |

### Typography Rules
- Headings use **sentence case** not title case, unless the headline is 4 words or fewer
- Body copy uses single space after full stops
- Do not justify body text — left-align only
- Avoid orphans (single word on last line) in headlines — rewrite the line
- Use en dashes (–) for ranges and em dashes (—) for parenthetical breaks, not hyphens

## 5. VOICE & TONE

### Brand Voice Attributes

| Attribute | What It Sounds Like | What It Does NOT Sound Like |
|---|---|---|
| **Authoritative** | "FP has trained over 30,000 professionals since 1999." | "We think we're pretty good at what we do." |
| **Clear** | "You pay $210 after the 70% IBF subsidy." | "Funding support may apply subject to eligibility." |
| **Encouraging** | "Your CFP® journey is closer than you think." | "This is a very difficult certification." |
| **Professional** | "Our curriculum meets FPSB global standards." | "Awesome! Super excited to share this!" |
| **Human** | "Study at midnight. Study on the MRT." | Corporate jargon, passive voice |

### Tone by Context

| Context | Tone |
|---|---|
| Landing pages | Confident, aspirational, urgent but not pushy |
| Email campaigns | Direct, professional, warm — one clear action per email |
| Social media | Accessible, insight-driven, occasionally conversational |
| FAQs / T&Cs | Plain English, factual, reassuring |
| Testimonials | Authentic, first-person, specific |
| Error messages / Notifications | Clear, helpful, never alarming |

## 6. WRITING STYLE RULES

### General Rules
- Write in **active voice**: "Register before seats close" not "Registration should be completed before seats are filled."
- Use **Singapore English** spellings: honour, recognise, organise, programme (not program, except for CFP® Programme which follows FPAS convention)
- Avoid filler phrases: "In order to", "Please be informed that", "It is important to note that"
- **One idea per sentence** in body copy. Short sentences outperform long ones.
- Never start a sentence with "However" as the first word of a paragraph
- Use numerals for all numbers in marketing copy: "3 modules", "25 years", "$210"
- Use the dollar symbol without spaces: $900, not $ 900 or S$ 900 (unless specifying Singapore dollars in a context with multiple currencies, use S$)

### Punctuation
- Use the Oxford comma in lists of three or more items
- Use em dashes (—) with spaces on both sides for parenthetical clauses
- Avoid exclamation marks except in event announcements and social media — maximum one per piece of content
- Use curly/smart quotes " " not straight quotes " "

### Calls to Action (CTAs)
CTAs must be:
- **Action-first**: Start with a verb — "Register", "Download", "WhatsApp Us", "Find Out More"
- **Specific**: "Register for Cycle 2" not just "Click Here"
- **Single per section**: Never put two CTAs of equal weight side by side

| ✅ Good CTA | ❌ Avoid |
|---|---|
| Register for CFP® Cycle 2 | Click Here |
| Download the Schedule | Submit |
| WhatsApp Us Now | Learn More |
| Secure Your Place Today | Find Out More (vague) |

## 7. PROGRAMME & CERTIFICATION NAMING CONVENTIONS

This section is critical. Always use the correct legal/official naming for certifications.

### Certification Marks
| Correct Usage | Notes |
|---|---|
| **CFP®** | Always include the ® registered mark symbol |
| **CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™** | All caps, with ™ trademark symbol |
| **AFP®** | Associate Financial Planner — include ® |
| **AWP^CM** | Associate Wealth Planner — CM as superscript |
| **HRAPP™** | Holistic Retirement Adequacy Planning Professional |
| **HELP** | Holistic Estate & Legacy Planning — no ® or ™ |
| **MFA** | Master Fund Adviser (note: Adviser, not Advisor) |
| **SCFP-STEPP** | Segmentation & Targeting for Effective Positioning & Propositions |

### Programme Names (FP Internal)
| Short Form | Full Name |
|---|---|
| CFP Programme | CFP® Certification Education Program |
| Self-Study | Online self-study (asynchronous e-learning) |
| Tutorial | Classroom tutorial option (in-person or Zoom) |
| Module 1 / M1 | Module 1A — Financial Planning Fundamentals (AFP Certification) |
| Module 2 / M2 | Module 2A — Risk Management & Insurance Planning |
| Module 3 / M3 | Module 3A — Tax Planning & Estate Planning |
| Module 4 / M4 | Module 4A — Investment Planning |
| Module 5 / M5 | Module 5A — Retirement Planning |
| Module 6 / M6 | Module 6A — Financial Plan Construction & Professional Responsibilities |

### Funding Scheme Names
| Short Form | Full Name | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| IBF-STS | IBF Standards Training Scheme | CFP® Programme, MFA, HELP |
| IBF-FTS | IBF Financial Training Scheme | SME Masterclass modules |
| SSG | SkillsFuture Singapore | Referenced in funding eligibility |

### Organisation Names
| Short Form | Full Name |
|---|---|
| FP | Financial Perspectives Pte Ltd |
| FPAS | Financial Planning Association of Singapore |
| FPSB | Financial Planning Standards Board Ltd |
| IBF | Institute of Banking & Finance |

### Rules
- Always spell out the full organisation name on first mention in a document, with short form in parentheses
- Always use "CFP®" (not "CFP", "CFP certificate", or "CFP qualification") — the ® mark is required by FPSB/FPAS licensing agreement
- "Programme" (British spelling) for the education programme; "Program" only within the official name "CFP® Certification Education Program" (follows FPAS convention)
- "Adviser" not "Advisor" when referring to financial advisers in Singapore context

## 8. EMAIL MARKETING STANDARDS

### Subject Line Formula
```
[Urgency/Number/Hook] + [Specific Benefit] + [Deadline or Action]
```
**Examples:**
- `Last Call: CFP® 77th Intake Cycle 2 Registration Closes Soon`
- `Up to 70% IBF Funding — Self-Study CFP® Now Available`
- `4 Courses. One Month. April CPD Calendar Is Here.`

### Email Structure
Every marketing email must follow this order:
1. **Pre-header text** (50–90 characters) — complements subject line, shows in inbox preview
2. **Header** — FP logo, centred, on white or navy background
3. **Hero section** — single headline + 1–2 sentence intro
4. **Body** — course/offer details, benefits
5. **CTA button** — one primary gold button, centred
6. **Secondary content** (optional) — supporting info, dates, pricing
7. **Footer** — contact details, unsubscribe link, legal disclaimer

### Email Formatting Rules
- Maximum width: **600px**
- Minimum font size: **14px** (16px preferred for body)
- Button minimum size: **44px height**, full-width on mobile
- All images must have **alt text**
- Every email must have a **plain text version**
- Line spacing in body: **1.6 minimum**
- Unsubscribe link: mandatory, in footer, must be visible (not hidden in grey tiny text)
- Never send without **preview text** set

### Mailchimp-Specific Notes
- Always tag subscribers by course interest and intake cycle for segmentation
- Use merge tags for personalisation: `*|FNAME|*`
- A/B test subject lines on audiences > 500 — test send time and subject line, not both simultaneously
- Send time: **Tuesday–Thursday, 9–10am SGT** performs best for the FP audience
- Avoid Mondays and Fridays for campaign sends

## 9. SOCIAL MEDIA STANDARDS

### Active Platforms
| Platform | Primary Purpose | Posting Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Facebook | Course promotion, lead generation, community | 3–4x per week |
| LinkedIn | Thought leadership, B2B, adviser audience | 2–3x per week |
| Instagram | Brand awareness, student success, visual content | 2x per week |
| WhatsApp Broadcast | Direct enrolment enquiries, event reminders | As needed |

### Post Structure (Facebook & LinkedIn)
```
[Hook — 1 line, no more than 10 words]

[Body — 2–4 short paragraphs, each 1–3 sentences]

[CTA — 1 action line]

[Link]

[Hashtags — LinkedIn: 3–5 | Facebook: 3–5]
```

### Hashtag Bank (approved)
```
#CFP #CertifiedFinancialPlanner #CPD #IBFFunded #FinancialAdviser
#FinancialPlanning #SingaporeFinance #SkillsFuture #ProfessionalDevelopment
#FinancialEducation #FPAS #SMEMasterclass #EstatePlanning #RetirementPlanning
#InvestmentPlanning #MasterFundAdviser #FinancialPerspectives
```

### Social Media Tone Rules
- Write to a **single person**, not a crowd: "If you're a financial adviser…" not "For financial advisers…"
- Lead with **insight or a question**, not the course name
- Keep the first line punchy — it's the hook that shows before "See More"
- No ALL CAPS except for programme acronyms (CFP®, MFA, HELP)
- Emojis are permitted on Facebook and Instagram — use purposefully, max 3–4 per post
- LinkedIn: more restrained — max 1–2 emojis, only at the start of bullet points

## 10. LANDING PAGE STANDARDS

### Structure Principles
- **One goal per page**: every landing page has exactly one CTA
- No top navigation bar (removes exit routes for ad traffic)
- Sticky CTA bar on mobile (appears after hero scroll)
- Mobile-first design — test at 375px before desktop
- Page load target: **under 3 seconds** on mobile (4G connection)

### Section Order (Standard CFP® Course Landing Page)
1. Hero (above fold — CTA visible without scrolling on desktop)
2. Urgency/funding callout bar (full-width, gold)
3. Problem/insight section
4. Video embed
5. Programme details / modules
6. Feature comparison or benefits
7. Pricing & funding table
8. Testimonials
9. Trust/about section
10. FAQ (accordion)
11. Lead capture form + final CTA
12. Footer (minimal — disclaimer only)

### CTA Button Standards
| Property | Specification |
|---|---|
| Background | FP Gold `#C9A84C` |
| Text colour | White `#FFFFFF` |
| Font | DM Sans, 600 weight |
| Font size | 16px desktop / 18px mobile |
| Padding | 16px 32px desktop / 18px full-width mobile |
| Border radius | 4px |
| Hover state | Darken gold by 10%: `#B8963E` |
| Shadow | `0 4px 14px rgba(201,168,76,0.35)` |

### Form Standards
- Minimum fields: Name, Email, Phone
- Always include PDPA consent checkbox (mandatory for Singapore)
- Label fields above the input (not placeholder-only)
- Show success message after submission — do not just clear the form
- Fire Facebook Pixel `Lead` event on successful submission

## 11. IMAGERY & VISUAL DIRECTION

### Photography Style
- **People**: Professional Singaporean financial professionals, diverse, 30–55 age range, business attire — not stock-photo-obvious
- **Environments**: Clean office settings, training rooms, Singapore CBD/High Street Centre context
- **Avoid**: Generic handshake photos, laptop-on-beach clichés, overly staged "pointing at whiteboard" images

### Illustration & Graphics
- Use geometric, structured graphic elements consistent with the brand's precision and authority
- Gold rule lines and dividers are preferred over full-colour graphic blocks
- Data visualisations (charts, comparison tables) should use FP Navy and FP Gold only

### Image Overlay Text Rule
- When placing text over photography, always use a **dark navy overlay** at 60–75% opacity
- Minimum contrast ratio for text over images: **4.5:1** (WCAG AA)

### Asset Formats
| Asset Type | Format | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Web images | WebP (with JPG fallback) | Compress to under 150KB per image |
| Logo | SVG (preferred) / PNG transparent | Never JPEG |
| Email images | JPG or PNG | Max 600px wide, under 100KB |
| Print assets | PDF (press-ready) | 300dpi minimum, CMYK |
| Social images | PNG or JPG | Platform-specific sizes below |

### Social Image Sizes
| Platform | Format | Size |
|---|---|---|
| Facebook Feed | Square or Landscape | 1080×1080 or 1200×630 |
| Facebook Story / Ad | Vertical | 1080×1920 |
| LinkedIn Post | Landscape | 1200×627 |
| LinkedIn Story | Vertical | 1080×1920 |
| Instagram Feed | Square | 1080×1080 |
| Email Header | Landscape | 600×200 |
| Banner / Standee | Various | As per print spec |

## 12. ICONOGRAPHY & UI ELEMENTS

### Icon Style
- Line icons preferred (stroke weight: 1.5–2px)
- Rounded corners consistent with the brand's approachable professionalism
- Colour: FP Navy or FP Gold only — never multicolour icon sets

### Badge / Tag Style
Used for: "IBF-STS Funded", "CPD Hours", "New", "Cycle 2 Open"
- Background: FP Navy (dark) or FP Gold (highlight)
- Text: White
- Shape: Pill (border-radius: 999px) or flat rectangle (border-radius: 4px)
- Font: DM Sans, all caps, 600 weight, 11–12px

### Dividers
- Thin horizontal rule: 1px solid `#C9A84C` (gold) — used between major sections
- Section separator: 2px FP Gold left border on callout boxes

### Card Style
- Background: White `#FFFFFF`
- Border: 1px solid `#E5E7EB`
- Border radius: 8px
- Shadow: `0 2px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.07)`
- Padding: 24px desktop / 20px mobile
